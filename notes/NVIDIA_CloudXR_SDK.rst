NVIDIA_CloudXR_SDK
==================

* https://docs.nvidia.com/cloudxr-sdk/latest/index.html


NVIDIA CloudXR™ is a GPU-accelerated XR streaming platform that enables
high-fidelity XR experiences to be streamed from powerful servers to
lightweight client devices over standard networks. CloudXR decouples rendering
from display; servers do the heavy computation, and clients just receive video
and send tracking data.


Windows/Linux servers to

* Apple Vision Pro
* iPhone/iPad
* Meta Quest 2/3/3S
* Pico 4 Ultra
* Web browsers
